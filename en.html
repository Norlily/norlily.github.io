Services

What we offer

Educational Consultancy

Emotional Support

Legal Advice

Networking
Guidance on the Dutch education system

We provide detailed advice on the Dutch education system, helping parents understand and navigate the schooling process to ensure their children's academic success.

Emotional support for families

We offer emotional support sessions for parents and children, helping them manage stress and adapt to a new culture and environment. Our services include individual and group consultations, recreational activities, and wellness workshops.

Legal advice and parents' rights

We provide information on the legal rights and responsibilities of parents in the Netherlands. We offer educational resources to help parents fully understand their rights and duties, helping them navigate the system with confidence and security.

Creating a network of contacts

We facilitate the creation of networks between newly arrived parents and those already established, promoting mutual support and social integration. Our goal is to create a supportive community that helps families adapt to their new environment, offering emotional and practical support throughout the integration process.
